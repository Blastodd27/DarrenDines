About Me

Food lover, frequent flyer, and firm believer that the best way to understand a place is to eat there.

My Story

Hi, I'm Darren.

I'm a graduate student studying international affairs at UC San Diego. When I'm not doing school work or messing around with my telescope, my mind usually goes straight to the next meal. Or more specifically, the next trip built around one.

This blog started as a personal journal. It was just a way to remember the dishes that made me stop mid-bite, look up, and think "I need to look back on this". Over time it became something more. It became a record of the world, eaten one city at a time.

I'm not a chef. I'm not a food critic. I'm just an person who believes deeply that sitting down to eat in a real place with real locals is the closest thing to actually understanding it. Every bowl of kari laksa, every dry-aged steak, and every slow-cooked stew has a story behind it. I try to tell those stories.

Whether I'm hunting for the perfect hawker stall in a London basement or taking a taxi to a Michelin-starred cottage in rural Ireland, the meal is just as much about the journey as the ingredients. Thanks for reading.
