Tacos Al Pastor at El Huequito

February 10, 2026  ¬∑  5 min read

The birthplace of tacos al pastor. Juicy marinated pork carved right off the trompo, piled onto warm handmade tortillas with fresh pineapple, white onion, and the best salsa verde I've ever tasted. Mexico City gave me a lot of great meals. This one was the first and the most unforgettable.
